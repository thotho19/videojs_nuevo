Keyboard shortcuts

  • Space bar - toggle play/pause.
  • Right and Left Arrow - seek the video forwards and back.
  • Up and Down Arrow - increase and decrease the volume.
  • M key - toggle mute/unmute.
  • F key - toggle fullscreen off and on.

Code Source

<!--Load Videojs CSS stylesheet in your website Head tag-->
<link href="../videojs/css/videojs.min.css" rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" />

<!--Load player and plugins-->
<script src="../videojs//video.min.js"></script>
<script src="../videojs//nuevo.min.js"></script>
<script src="../videojs//plugins/videojs.hotkeys.min.js"></script>

<!--Setup Video tag-->
<video id="player_1" class="video-js vjs-fluid vjs-default-skin" controls preload playsinline poster="images/poster.jpg">
	<source src="//devnuevo.com/media/video/demo_720.mp4" type="video/mp4">
	<source src="//devnuevo.com/media/video/demo_720.webm" type="video/mp4">
</video>

<script>
<!--Initialize player + nuevo and hotkeys plugins-->
<script>
	var player = videojs('player_1');
	player.nuevo({ 
		title: "Nuevo plugin for VideoJs Player"
	}); 
	player.hotkeys({
		volumeStep: 0.1,
		seekStep: 5
	});
</script>

Hotkeys options are optional and easy to understand.
volumeStep = step value to increse/decrease volume.
seekStep = step value to forward or backeard video in time.
